About

My name is Ryan.

I’m an aspiring Developer based in Cardiff with a passion for Web and Mobile Developemnt. Eager to create modern, unique, and intuitive systems.

I graduated from the University of South Wales with a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems achieving an upper second class honours.

As a child I always enjoyed making things and taking things apart while also gravitating towards technology and computers as I got older. And ever since saving up the money to build my own PC I’ve always wanted to do something with computers. But it wasn’t until taking Computing class at A-Level that I realised what I enjoyed was programming and using code to create things. Since then I have learnt a lot through multiple university projects and investing my spare time into bettering my knowledge of different technologies.
